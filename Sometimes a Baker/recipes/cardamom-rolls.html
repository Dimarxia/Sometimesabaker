Cardamom Rolls

Pillowy soft, warmly spiced, glazed with pearl-sugar crunch

Prep 35 min
Proof 2–3 hr
Bake 18 min
Total ~3.5 hr
Yield 12 rolls
Difficulty ⭑⭑⭒
⚖ Serving Scaler
12
Base recipe = 12 rolls. All weights below update automatically.
🌾 Ingredients
Enriched Dough
  • Whole milk warmed to 38 °C 240 ml
  • Fast-action dried yeast 7 g
  • Caster sugar 50 g
  • Strong white bread flour 450 g
  • Ground cardamom freshly ground, green pods 8 g
  • Fine sea salt 6 g
  • Unsalted butter softened, cubed 75 g
  • Egg large, room temperature 55 g (~1 large)
Cardamom Butter Filling
  • Unsalted butter softened 80 g
  • Light brown sugar packed 80 g
  • Ground cardamom freshly ground 6 g
  • Ground cinnamon optional, supports the cardamom 3 g
Egg Wash & Topping
  • Egg beaten 55 g (1 large)
  • Whole milk 15 ml
  • Pearl sugar or crushed sugar cubes 30 g
📋 Method

  1. Activate the yeast. Combine warm milk (38 °C), yeast, and 10 g of the caster sugar in the KitchenAid bowl. Whisk briefly and rest uncovered.

    ⏱ Rest 10:00 ✓ Done

    💡 The mixture should become foamy and fragrant. If it doesn't, your yeast may be expired.

  2. Mix the dough. To the foamy yeast mixture, add the remaining caster sugar, egg, flour, cardamom, and salt. Fit the dough hook. Mix on speed 2 for 3 minutes until a shaggy dough forms.

    ⏱ Mix 03:00 ✓ Done

  3. Add butter. With the mixer on speed 2, add the softened butter cube by cube over 2–3 minutes. Once incorporated, increase to speed 4 and knead until the dough is smooth, elastic, and pulls cleanly from the bowl sides.

    ⏱ Knead 08:00 ✓ Done

    💡 Do a windowpane test: stretch a small piece — it should be thin enough to be nearly translucent without tearing.
